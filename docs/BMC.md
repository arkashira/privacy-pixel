# Business Model Canvas for Privacy Pixel

## Value Proposition

### Unique Selling Points

*   **Data Privacy**: Our solution ensures that images are exported without compromising sensitive data, providing users with peace of mind.
*   **Format Flexibility**: We support a wide range of image formats, catering to diverse user needs.
*   **Easy Integration**: Our Python library simplifies the process of exporting images, making it accessible to developers and non-technical users alike.

### Key Benefits

*   **Security**: Protect sensitive data by avoiding metadata exposure.
*   **Convenience**: Effortlessly export images in various formats.
*   **Customization**: Tailor image export to specific requirements with our flexible library.

## Customer Segments

### Primary Target Market

*   **Developers**: Python developers working on projects that require image processing and export.
*   **Data Analysts**: Professionals handling sensitive data who need to export images without compromising security.
*   **Business Owners**: Entrepreneurs and small business owners who require image export for marketing and promotional purposes.

### Secondary Target Market

*   **Researchers**: Scientists and researchers who need to export images for academic and research purposes.
*   **Artists**: Graphic designers, photographers, and artists who require image export for creative projects.

## Channels

### Primary Distribution Channels

*   **GitHub**: Our primary distribution channel for the Python library.
*   **PyPI**: We will also distribute the library through the Python Package Index (PyPI).
*   **Documentation**: Comprehensive documentation will be available on our GitHub repository and website.

### Secondary Distribution Channels

*   **Online Communities**: We will engage with online communities, such as Reddit's r/learnpython and r/Python, to promote our library.
*   **Social Media**: We will utilize social media platforms, like Twitter and LinkedIn, to reach a broader audience.

## Revenue Streams

### Primary Revenue Streams

*   **GitHub Sponsorships**: We will offer GitHub Sponsors to support our development and maintenance efforts.
*   **Consulting Services**: We will provide consulting services to help clients implement our library in their projects.
*   **Training and Workshops**: We will offer training and workshops to help developers and data analysts learn how to use our library effectively.

### Secondary Revenue Streams

*   **Affiliate Marketing**: We will partner with affiliate programs to earn commissions from sales of related products and services.
*   **Data Analytics**: We will offer data analytics services to help clients make informed decisions about their image export needs.
